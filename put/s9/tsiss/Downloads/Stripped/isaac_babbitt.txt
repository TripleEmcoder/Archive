   Known for [9]Babbitt metal

   Isaac Babbitt (b. July 26, 1799, [10]Taunton, Massachusetts - d. May
   26, 1862, [11]Somerville, Massachusetts) was in 1839 the inventor of a
   low-friction tin-based metal alloy, [12]Babbitt metal, that is used
   extensively in engine [13]bearings today.^[14][1]
