   Charles D. Seeberger (1857–1931) was an American inventor. In 1899, he
   joined the [10]Otis Elevator Company. The Seeberger-Otis partnership
   produced the first step-type [11]escalator made for public use, and it
   was installed at the [12]Paris Exhibition of 1900, where it won first
   prize. Mr. Seeberger eventually sold his [13]patent rights to Otis in
   1910.
