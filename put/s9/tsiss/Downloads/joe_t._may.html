Joe T. May (born June 8, 1937, in Broadway, Virginia) is an American politician of the Republican Party. Since 1994 he has been a member of the Virginia House of Delegates. He currently[update] represents the 33rd district, made up of Clarke County and part of Loudoun County.[1] He is also an engineer and inventor who holds over 20 patents in the fields of electrical and electronic engineering.[2]
